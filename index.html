About Us
Introduction
This Organization was founded by Mr. Amrit Sapkota in 2021 with other 3 co-founders with the aim to encourage sports in university level.
Objective
The main obejctive of this organization is to promote and support sports activities in university level.
Our Priority
To build sports environment in the universities across the country.
Achievements
This organization has achieved many awards in sports field and dedicated to provide sports scholarship to many potential students.
Future Plan
This organization aims to promote healthy lifestyle, sports for peace and development among youths worldwide.
Location
The main office of this organization is located at Nayabazar-9, Pokhara, Nepal. It has other branches across the country. Jan 29,2021.
