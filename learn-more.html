Champ Sports Club

Champ Sports Club has been running this organization since 1990 in order to promote sports to university students and other helpless people and not only that but also to encourage people to participate in sports more!

Learn more about us

"One team, One Dream"

Our Origin

We were a very small organization when it was established. It was a very hard time for us to continue doing our social work because we had so little participation. There was a time when our organization was also disbanded.

Our Motive

All of the members of the organization are driven to provide better sports facilities to all the students as best as we can. We fight for the right to sports for all the students in the nation so they can enjoy sports freely from their heart.

Our Current Position

We are now the top leading sports organization to support and encourage sports in universities all over Nepal. We were only able to achieve this thanks to our strong campaign all over the nation with the help of our supporters.
